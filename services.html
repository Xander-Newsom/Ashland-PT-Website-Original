Services We Offer

At our clinic, we provide comprehensive care for individuals across all stages of life from children to college students/athletes, working adults, and seniors. Our experienced team treats a wide range of conditions, including, but not limited to, orthopedic injuries, stroke recovery, traumatic brain injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, chronic pain. No matter your age or diagnosis, we are dedicated to delivering personalized, effective therapy that supports your health and helps you regain function and independence. Several of the services we provide are listed below.

  • Chronic pain management
  • Balance and fall prevention
  • Vertigo and vestibular rehabilitation
  • Manual therapy
  • Therapeutic exercise
  • Orthopedic and sports injury rehabilitation
  • Post surgical recovery
  • Wellness
  • Dry Needling
  • Cupping
