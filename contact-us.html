301 S. Railroad Ave, Ashland, VA 23005

Ashland PT is located at 301 S. Railroad Ave, Ashland, VA 23005. It may also be found listed as 301 S. Center Street. If you are coming from I-95 or Route 1 toward Ashland, travel west on Route 54. Turn left on Virginia Street, and then right on Myrtle Street. At S. Railroad Ave (also S. Center St) you will only be able to turn right, unless you cross the railroad tracks.

If traveling from points west along Route 54, you will turn right on Virginia Street, just past downtown Ashland. Turn right on Myrtle and then right on Railroad Ave (Center St).

Our clinic is located on the right side just after the turn onto Railroad Ave. There are designated handicap parking spots located next to the clinic by turning right on Lee Street.

The main entrance to the building is located along Lee Street, and is handicap accessible. If dropping off patients, you may briefly pull up outside the door along Lee Street.
