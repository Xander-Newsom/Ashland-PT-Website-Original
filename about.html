About Us

Ashland PT is a private, employee owned, physical therapy practice in the heart of downtown Ashland, Virginia.

As a privately owned small business, we are committed to serving the surrounding communities by providing evidence based, compassionate care. Our commitment as therapists is to help you move better, feel stronger, and live with less pain. Located along the railroad tracks in Ashland, we offer a small town atmosphere with high quality, personalized care, where your goals are our priority.

Ashland PT is founded on the belief that quality care should be one-on-one and hands on, and our clinic provides tailored treatment plans to meet your individual needs. Whether you are recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, or striving to improve your quality of life, we are here to support you every step of your journey.

At Ashland PT, we value connection, communication, and trust. We take the time to listen, understand, and educate, so you are not just a recipient of care, but an active participant in your care.
